Our Story

50 Years of Fresh

In 1967, we opened our doors in Abilene, TX to share fresh, authentic Mexican food. Now, almost 50 years
later, while the other guys have turned to packaged and processed foods, we’ve stayed true to our roots
by crafting authentic Tex-Mex recipes in each of our kitchens. We do things the Bueno way, and you’re
sure to taste the difference.

President & CEO

MIKE ROPER

Mike has been a part of Bueno since 2013. He’s served as
President, Chief Operating Officer and Chief Technology &
Development Officer, so he knows his way around our tacos.
Before becoming a part of team Bueno, Mike served as President
of IMS Southern Colorado. From 2003 to 2012, Mike was a
sandwich man at Quizno’s Subs, serving as Chief Operating
Officer, Executive Vice President of Restaurant Operations, Senior
Vice President of Restaurant Operations and three other VP
positions. From 2000 to 2006, Mike was a Multi-Unit Franchisee of
Quizno’s Subs and owned four restaurants in Chicago, IL.

CFO

PHILIP PARSONS

Philip has been our CFO since 2008. Before joining team Bueno,
Philip was a Partner with Tatum LLC, where he provided strategic
and turnaround services for brands like Fuddruckers, Steak & Ale
and Bennigan’s. From 2003 to 2005, Philip served as the CFO of
iDefense, Inc., a provider of global cyber security intelligence. He
has also served in both operational and financial planning and
analysis roles for KFC International based in Sydney, Australia.

CMO

SARAH BEDDOE

Sarah Beddoe joined Taco Bueno in 2017 as Chief Marketing
Officer. From 2015—2017, Sarah served as Vice President of
National Marketing for SONIC, where she focused on strategic
marketing planning, the marketing calendar and an innovative
product pipeline. Prior to SONIC, Beddoe was Director of Digital
Experience and Social Engagement for Pizza Hut Corp. Sarah
started her career in finance with Neiman Marcus Group in Dallas.

VP Franchise

MATTHEW STANTON

Matt came on board in 2016 and oversees real estate selection
and franchise development. Before joining team Bueno, Matt
served as VP of Strategy, Real Estate, and Franchise Development
at Smashburger, helping grow the brand from 100 to 360 units. His
prior experiences include running growth strategy and acquisitions
for a division of Celanese, a global technology and specialty
materials company, and working as a management consultant with
Bain & Company focused on growth strategy for clients in a variety of industries.
