TACOS

The Original Crispy Taco

The Original Way to Bueno

This Bueno classic is how we got our name! A crunchy corn tortilla cradles your choice of lightly seasoned ground beef, all white meat chicken or fajita steak, along with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes and freshly grated cheddar cheese for a taste that’s nothing but Bueno.

Soft Taco

A Flour Tortilla Packed with Flavor

Taste the softer side of this Bueno classic. Our soft taco comes with all your favorite fillings, including your choice of fresh ground beef, all white meat chicken or fajita steak, along with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes and grated cheddar cheese, carefully wrapped in a fresh-made flour tortilla.

The Muchaco®

Bueno’s Best Kept Secret

This Bueno exclusive takes tacos to a whole new level. We swapped the shell with warm, fluffy pita bread and stuffed it with slow-cooked refried beans, your choice of all white meat chicken, fajita steak or seasoned ground beef, along with shredded cheddar cheese, crisp lettuce and juicy tomatoes.

Big Freak’n Taco

A Bigger Way to Bueno

One taco is never enough, so we packed double the flavor and freshness into this two-handed crispy corn tortilla. Enjoy two times the fresh, never frozen ground beef, all white meat chicken or fajita steak, plus grated cheddar cheese with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes and a dollop of cool sour cream.

BURRITOS

Bueno Burrito

A Burrito the Bueno Way

Wrap your taste buds around five layers of everything Bueno. We start with slow-cooked refried beans and layer in flavorful Mexican rice, chili sauce, grated cheddar cheese and your choice of seasoned ground beef, all white meat chicken or juicy fajita steak.

The BOB - Big Ol’ Burrito

Meet BOB

A burrito so big, it needed its own acronym. We upgraded our fresh flour tortilla to a size that can hold extra fillings. Your choice of seasoned ground beef, juicy fajita steak or all white meat chicken is layered with spicy chili sauce, grated cheddar cheese, crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes and cool sour cream to make this Bueno-famous burrito.

Bean Burrito

A Better Way to Burrito

Everyone should enjoy a little Bueno, so we created this tasty option packed with fat-free, vegetarian black beans, pepper jack cheese, cilantro-lime rice and refreshing sour cream. It’s all wrapped up in a soft flour tortilla that delivers flavor straight to your taste buds.
