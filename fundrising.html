Fundrising

Doing Something Bueno for the Community

Eat Tacos. Get Money.

We want to help your organization raise the money it needs to be
successful. We know a good cause and great taste are the perfect
pair, so we’ve made it easy to host a fundraising night at Taco
Bueno, where your group will receive 15% of net sales.

It's easy to get started

JUST FOLLOW THESE STEPS:

GET IN TOUCH

Submit your request online, and we’ll work with you to create a fundraising night
that fits your needs.

SPREAD THE WORD

Then, we’ll give you a template for posters and invitations. Invite as many people
as you can, and dedicate a few members of your group to actively promoting
your event.

EAT SOME TACOS

Head to Taco Bueno for a night of flavor and fundraising! Be sure to let us know
the name of your group when you order, and tell all your guests to do the same.

ADD IT UP

After your event, we’ll count the receipts from people who mentioned your
group to calculate your fundraising total.

GET YOUR CHECK

Finally, we’ll send you a check for 15% of the net sales (excluding tax).

That’s it! Taco Bueno fundraising nights are an easy and delicious way to raise money for your group.
We can’t wait to do a little Bueno for your group and community.
